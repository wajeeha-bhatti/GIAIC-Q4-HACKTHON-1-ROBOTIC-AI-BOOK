# Humanoid Anatomy

Humanoid robotics ki anatomy insani jism se inspired hoti hai. Is chapter ka maqsad aapko samjhana hai ke humanoid robot kis tarah design hota hai, kaun kaun se core components hotay hain, aur har hissa robot ki functioning me kya role ada karta hai.

---

## **1. Introduction to Humanoid Anatomy**

Humanoid robots insaan jaisi structure rakhte hain — head, torso, arms, hands, legs, aur sometimes facial expressions. Unka design unhe human environments me asani se kaam karne ke qabil banata hai.

---

## **2. Core Structural Components**

### **2.1 Head (Sensor Hub)**

Humanoid robot ka head sensors ka center hota hai:

* Cameras for vision
* Depth sensors
* Microphones
* IMU for orientation

Head ka purpose environment ko perceive karna hota hai.

---

### **2.2 Torso (Central Framework)**

Torso robot ka core structural frame hota hai:

* Battery placement
* Cooling systems
* AI computer module
* Balance controllers

Yeh robot ka stability center hota hai.

---

### **2.3 Arms & Shoulders (Manipulation System)**

Arms ka kaam objects ko pick, hold, lift, aur use karna hota hai.

Components:

* Shoulder joint (3 DOF)
* Elbow joint (1–2 DOF)
* Wrist joint (3 DOF)
* High-power actuators

Humanoid arm design insani movement se inspired hota hai.

---

### **2.4 Hands & Fingers (End Effectors)**

Hands robots ke liye bohot important hote hain kyun ke yeh environment ke saath direct interact karte hain.

Features:

* Multi-finger dexterity
* Touch sensors
* Grip variations
* Fine motor control

Advanced humanoids (jaise Figure 01) human-like precision hasil karne ki koshish karte hain.

---

### **2.5 Legs & Feet (Locomotion System)**

Legs humanoid ko chalne, daurne, jump karne aur terrain adapt karne ke qabil banati hain.

Parts:

* Hip joint
* Knee joint
* Ankle joint
* Force torque sensors

Feet flat ya curved ho sakte hain stability ke hisaab se.

---

## **3. Internal Systems**

### **3.1 Actuation System**

* Electric motors
* Hydraulics
* Series elastic actuators

Yeh system robot ke movements control karta hai.

### **3.2 Sensory System**

* Vision sensors
* Proximity sensors
* Force sensors
* Gyroscope / IMU

### **3.3 Control System**

Robot ke andar multiple computers hotay hain:

* High-level AI
* Low-level motor controller
* Safety controller

---

## **4. Balance & Stability**

Humanoids ka sab se challenging hissa balance hota hai.

Isme use hotay hain:

* ZMP (Zero Moment Point)
* Dynamic gait planning
* Real-time feedback loops

---

## **5. Why Humanoid Anatomy Matters?**

* Human environments me seamless operation
* Human tool usage compatibility
* Natural communication & interaction
* Adaptive locomotion

---

## **Conclusion**

Humanoid anatomy insani structure ka robotic version hai — sensors, motors, AI systems aur mechanics ka combination jo robot ko insaan jaisi functionality deta hai. Yeh chapter future modules ke liye base provide karta hai, jisme movement, perception aur intelligence deeply cover honge.
